Corporate & Civil Litigation

"Discourage Litigation. Persuade your neighbours to compromise whenever you can. As a peacemaker the lawyer has superior opportunity of being a good man. There will still be business enough." – Abraham Lincoln

TKP Law’s legal team is well versed in corporate and civil litigation and has the experience to assist a client in most legal situations. We take pride in our ability to closely examine each client’s current situation and operational stance. Thereafter, we cater our approach and strategy accordingly to address each client’s needs at that point in time.

TKP Law distinguishes itself from other law firms through the attention provided to each client. We strive to address issues rapidly and effectively as they arise so that you can keep your attention on having your operations grow, unencumbered by legal concerns.

TKP Law’s approach to assigning 2 Partners, together with support staff to every litigation dispute creates a unique synergy that enables TKP Law to provide skilled and experienced representation in virtually any civil dispute.

Corporate and Civil Litigation encompasses a broad range of disputes, and TKP Law prides itself in specialization in, inter alia, the following practice areas: -

  • Wrongful Termination Suits and Labour Disputes
  • Litigating Shareholder Derivative Actions
  • Breach of Contract and Sale of Goods Issues
  • Insurance and Personal Injury Claims
  • Corporate Real Estate Issues
  • Commercial Landlord and Tenant Matters
  • Negligence Claims
  • Insolvencies and Receiverships
  • Construction Disputes
  • Maritime Law
  • Will Contests and Disputes
  • Libel, Defamation and Slander Suits
  • Injunction Applications

However, while many of the firm’s lawyers specialize in specific types of civil litigation, TKP Law and its legal team hold a unique level of flexibility that enables them to litigate virtually any type of case.
