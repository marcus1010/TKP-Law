James Joshua Paulraj

  • Email: james@tkplaw.co

James graduated with a Bachelor of Laws (LLB) (Honours) from the University of Leeds in 2010. In the same year, he pursued the Certificate of Legal Practice and was awarded the same in 2011. He read in the Chambers of Messrs. Kiru & Yong, under the direct guidance of Dato’ K. Kirubakaran from February to November 2012.

He was called to the Malaysian Bar on 11.01.2013 and worked as a legal associate in the firm of Rajadevan & Associates from February 2013 to March 2014 and subsequently in the firm of Raj, Ong & Yudistra from May 2014 to July 2015.

James is involved in commercial, corporate, civil litigation matters.
Some of the matters that James has been involved in include:

  • Lead counsel in the ongoing suit representing 1065 plaintiffs’ against Genneva Malaysia Sdn Bhd.
  • Litigation portfolio for Bandar Utama Sdn Bhd.
  • Counsel for the branch managers of Bank Rakyat in a claim for unlawful dismissal in the industrial court, and later High Court.
  • Construction disputes- Represented a main contractor in a dispute with regards to payment by sub-contractors (involved challenging the decision of Y.A. Dato’ Mary Lim in UDA Holdings with regards to Section 35 of the Construction Industry Payment and Adjudication Act 2012).
  • Corporate Shareholder disputes (included invoking the doctrine of agency of necessity in a claim by a shareholder).
  • Counsel for the suit by the Semai aboriginal people against TNB, JAKOA, State Government of Pahang and Government of Malaysia.
  • Counsel for BMTT Corporation Sdn Bhd up until the Federal Court in a claim in relation to the Sale of Goods Act.
  • Litigation portfolio for Pentas Flora Sdn Bhd (criminal and civil litigation).
  • Counsel for liquidator in the winding up of Chip Lam Seng Sdn Bhd.
  • Lead Counsel in the suit opposing the acquisition of land in Cameron Highlands for the PR1MA housing project.
  • Lead Counsel in a suit challenging Section 6 of the Distribution Act with regards to the rights of a child born out of wedlock to be entitled to a portion of the estate of the deceased.
  • Assisted in representing the former directors in the ongoing suit involving Protasco Berhad.
  • Assisted Mubashir Bin Mansor (equity partner in SKRINE) for Pelaburan MARA Berhad in a defamation claim.
  • Counsel for the Intellectual Property boutique practice of Mirandah Asia (Malaysia) in relation to IP disputes.

Other matters handled include:-

  • Personal Injury and other tortious claims (including but not limited to medical negligence and defamation claims).
  • Bankruptcy and Winding Up.
  • Debt recovery.
  • Garnishee and Charging Orders.
  • Injunctions
  • Appeals (High Court, Court of Appeal and Federal Court)
  • Judicial Review.
