Conveyancing

In understanding that private and corporate conveyancing are highly in demand in today's challenging business plethora.

TKP Law specializes in Conveyancing ranging from Sale and Purchase of Properties to various land matters. Our legal team at TKP, offer highly skilled and comprehensive advice on all conveyancing matters relating to both private and commercial property within Malaysia.

We believe that every house sold or bought should be hassle free and smooth sailing. TKP Law takes pride in maintaining high standards of professionalism and meticulousness in all conveyancing matters. Every client at TKP is greeted with a comprehensive understanding of a practical guide on ascertaining their dream home. We strive to provide reliable, fast and practical advice to all our clients whilst always maintaining the highest standards of professionalism.

We believe that a property investment should always be considered and whatever your destination, we at TKP Law will get you there faster and safer.

At TKP Law we specialize in the following Family Law practice areas: -

  • Sale and Purchase Agreements for Acquisition and Disposals
  • Private Caveats
  • Transfer of Property
  • Love and Affection Transactions
  • Discharge of Charge
  • Perfection of Memorandum of Transfer
  • Registration of Charge
  • Loan Documentation
  • Property Facility/Loan Agreements
  • Real Property Gains Tax
  • Application for Consent to Transfer
  • Applications for Foreign Property Acquisition/Disposal
  • Land Office related matters
